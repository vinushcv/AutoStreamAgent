# AutoStream Pricing & Features

## Basic Plan
- **Price**: $29/month
- **Features**:
    - 10 videos/month
    - 720p resolution

## Pro Plan
- **Price**: $79/month
- **Features**:
    - Unlimited videos
    - 4K resolution
    - AI captions

# Company Policies
- **Refunds**: No refunds after 7 days from purchase.
- **Support**: 24/7 priority support is available ONLY on the Pro plan. Basic plan users have email support with 48h turnaround.
